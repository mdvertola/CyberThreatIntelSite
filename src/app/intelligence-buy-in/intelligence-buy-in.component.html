Risks

Cost of attack ownership:
  • Magellan Health Inc. will have to take ownership of the breached records and be responsible for liabilities associated with the breach. A class action lawsuit was filed on June 29, 2020 which could potentially cost the company millions (Shaak, 2020)
  • Florida Orthopedic Institute was hacked by ransomware and their class action lawsuit cost $99 million because of the patient data exposure.
Pandemic:
  • Malicious actors will also continue to attack other healthcare companies because of strained resources due to the pandemic.
  • The FBI and two federal agencies said they have credible information of “an increased and imminent cybercrime threat” to U.S. hospitals and health care providers (Bajack, 2020)

Mitigation

Layered Security (Defense-in-Depth) uses control diversity implementing:
  • Administrative
  • Technical
  • Physical Security Controls
Additional Security layers to protect individual components of a system may include:
  • Access measures
  • Workstation defense
  • Data protection
  • Threat Intelligence
