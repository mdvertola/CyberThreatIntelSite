Cyber Threat Intelligence

Government Healthcare Sector

The Cybersecurity and Infrastructure Agency (CISA) identifies 16 key critical infrastructures, one of which is the Healthcare and Public Health Sector. The HPH Sector is large, diverse, and open, spanning both the public and private sectors. This includes publicly accessible healthcare facilities, research centers, and other physical assets. Vast information technology systems are required for rapid and secure transmission and storage of large amounts of HPH data.
